Second ARIAC WP1 meeting, 23/03/2022, online

The second ARIAC WP1 meeting will take place on Wednesday 23 March 2022, starting at 13:00. It will be mainly devoted the the Grand Challenges, and their connection to the WP1. We will therefore have presentations and discussions with the CRAs. We will also have contributions from ARIAC researchers about their work.

This meeting will be fully remote, via Teams. The link and practical information will be communicated in this page and in the mailing list in the next few days.
