ARIAC WP1 meeting 8/5/24, BeCentral

This academic year's meeting of the researchers working on or interested in the topics of the ARIAC WP1 on Human-AI interaction will take place on Wednesday 8 May afternoon at BeCentral, with support from FARI, the Brussels AI Institute for the Common Good.

BeCentral is situated at Cantersteen 10/12, 1000 Brussels, right above the Central Station (map), easily reachable from anywhere in Belgium.

Register at this link.

Meeting program

  • 12:00 - 13:00 lunch (for TRAIL researchers)
  • 13:00 - 15:00 contributed presentation from TRAIL researchers
    • Dani Manjah: Confirmation Bias in Stream-Based Active Distillation
    • Axel Abels: Resolving Knowledge Limitations for Improved Collective Intelligence
    • Jan Held: X-VARS: Introducing Explainability in Football Refereeing with Multi-Modal Large Language Model
    • Charlotte Nachtegael: DUVEL: an active-learning annotated biomedical corpus for the recognition of oligogenic combinations
    • Tim Bary: Multi-expert optimum sampling
    • Laura Gálvez Jiménez: Estimating self-consistency of experts in histopathology imaging using visual features
    • Natarajan Chidambaram: RABBIT: A CLI Tool for Identifying Bots Based on GitHub Events
  • 15:00 - 15:30 coffee break
  • 15:30 - 16:30 panel on supervision, responsibility and accountability in AI, with Vincenzo Tiani (PANETTA Studio Legale & VUB) and Naira López Cañellas (DIGITAL SME & TU Dublin)
  • 16:30 - 17:30 guided tour of the FARI AI Experience Center, with Carl Morch (FARI)
